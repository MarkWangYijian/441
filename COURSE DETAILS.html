Course recommendation

Noodles in Ginger Soup

Ginger noodle soup is a typical pasta snack in Taizhou, Zhejiang Province. It is mainly made of ginger juice or ginger soup. The ingredients in the soup are dried shrimp, rotten skin, mushrooms, day lily and other ingredients. The noodles are made of rice noodles. The ginger soup is full of flavor, spicy, and has a unique mellow flavor, but the body is warm and comfortable. Eating in summer can remove moisture.

Egg white sheep tail

Egg white sheep tail, also known as egg white mixed with sand, is a traditional famous food in Linhai, Taizhou, Zhejiang Province. This food is mainly made of egg white, bean paste, glutinous rice flour and other ingredients, and then fried. It looks like small balls, goose yellow, chubby, and tender. It tastes soft, sweet, and tender. The bean paste inside is delicate, moist, and smooth.

Wheat shrimp

Wheat shrimp is a delicious snack in the coastal area of Taizhou, Zhejiang Province. It is mainly made of flour paste and looks like shrimp, so it is named wheat shrimp instead of shrimp. Many foreigners feel a little confused when eating it for the first time. Isn't it just a bowl of crooked noodles? But when I eat it in my mouth, the feeling of tender and delicious is really wonderful and appetizing.

Cake bowl

Cake tube is a traditional snack in Taizhou, Zhejiang Province. It is a thin and tough cake skin, which is wrapped with egg skin, shredded potatoes, shredded radish, bean sprouts, celery, shredded meat, shredded bamboo shoots, mushrooms and other ingredients. Then it is rolled into a bamboo tube shape, gathering the fragrance of various ingredients. It has a sense of hierarchy when eating, and various delicacies are released in the mouth.

Wenling Cake

Wenling Cake is a special kind of New Year Cake that looks like dumplings. Walking in the streets and alleys of Wenling, we can say that there are many people selling embedded cakes everywhere. This kind of cake inlaying method is very simple. It is a kind of New Year Cake wrapped with a variety of side dishes to make a New Year Cake dumpling. It has rich fillings, and it is delicious to eat. The soft skin is wrapped with fresh, crisp and tender fillings, which is satisfying.

Pickled shrimp

Soaked shrimp is a special snack in Taizhou, Zhejiang Province. It is mainly a kind of food that is wrapped with shrimp, minced meat, eggs and other ingredients and fried. When it is hot, you can take a bite. It has a strong smell and chews crispy, not to mention delicious.
