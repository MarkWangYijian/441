Course recommendation

Business Administration

With our Certificate in Business Administration you’ll learn the advanced level administrative skills to manage and supervise others in any type of organisation: business, government, private and nonprofit. Want to lead with confidence? This course can help you advance your career and prepare you for managerial and top-tier administrative roles. Get equipped with the skills and knowledge to manage key information in any organisation.

Website development

This course contains a combination of coding and management units and is ideal to help you take your first steps into entering the website development industry, teaching you to create and manage basic websites from concept to launch. It also provides the requisites to undertake the Diploma that will take your learning to the next level with advanced concepts that allow you to create fully customised websites and hosting solutions.

Diploma of Information Technology

Learn knowledge and skills to design, analyse, implement and manage the cyber security strategies, frameworks, management processes and technical cyber security controls that minimise cyber security vulnerabilities and risks to business operations and practices. Our classes, courses and assessment are all created with you in mind; with experienced instructors, hands-on training that consider your unique situation.
